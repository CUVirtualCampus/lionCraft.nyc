Mission

Our goal is to provide current and former Columbia students with a virtual campus to reunite with classmates and professors in the Minecraft Virtual World. By working together as a community, users can help rebuild Columbia in all of its glory, block by block.

Inspiration

Everything started at the Columbia University VIRTUAL CAMPUS Design Challenge .

Students have expressed how disconnected they feel from campus as seen in multiple posts on Facebook.
In this way we are providing an opportunity for students to connect with each other “on campus” to combat these issues of loneliness and disconnectedness before the Fall through a fun interactive campus on Minecraft.
Most recently, Rachel Mow and J.T. Timpanaro have support our use of their Columbia map to create this Columbia community. We currently have an entire map of campus but many of the buildings are empty inside so we're hoping to work with campus to fill in the insides!

Other resources to connect with CU students: www.columbiavirtualcampus.com
