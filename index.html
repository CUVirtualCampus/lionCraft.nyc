Mission

Our goal is to provide current and former Columbia students with a virtual campus to reunite with classmates and professors. By working together as a community, users can help rebuild Columbia in all of its glory, block by block.

Join us

Design for the columbia community (incoming student)!

1 - Join the Slack channel at columbiavirtualcampus.slack.com

2 - Send a message to #newuser channel with your columbia email and your minecraft username! (so we can grant you the right rank!) (or email elt2141@columbia.edu if you don't have Slack)

3 - Receive a reply message with the server's ip on your email! (have fun)

4 - Upon joining the server you'll be granted the rank of Student can participate in Student Projects
